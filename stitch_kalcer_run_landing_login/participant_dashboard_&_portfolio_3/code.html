info About Net Time vs Gun Time

Net Time (Chip Time) is measured from the moment you cross the start mat to the moment you cross the finish mat. Official Time (Gun Time) is measured from the start signal. Prizes are usually based on Gun Time.

military_tech E-Certificate & Medal

Official digital certificates are available for download 24 hours after verification. Physical finisher medals for virtual runs will be shipped within 7 days of completion. Keep running, pelari kalcer!
